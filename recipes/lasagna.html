Lasagna

Description

Trying to make a quick and easy lasagna? Here is a simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 pound proteins (italian sausage, chicken, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • 2 cans of canned tomato sauce (6.5 oz)
  • 2 cans of tomato paste (6 oz)
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • A good amount of mozzarella and parmesan
  • Some parsley, basil, salt, black pepper, and other seasonings
