Lasagna

Description

Trying to make a quick and easy lasagna? Here is a simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 pound proteins (italian sausage, chicken, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • 2 cans of canned tomato sauce (6.5 oz)
  • 2 cans of tomato paste (6 oz)
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • A good amount of mozzarella and parmesan
  • Some parsley, basil, salt, black pepper, and other seasonings

Directions

  1. Cook the proteins, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well-browned
  2. Stir in crusedh tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Add sugar, half of your parsley, basil, salt, seasoning, and pepper. Simmer this covered for 1.5 hours, stirring ocassionally.
  3. Boil some lightly salted water and cook the lasagna noodles for 8-10 minutes. Rinse with cold water.
  4. In another bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with egg, the rest of your parsley, and salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  6. To assemble on a baking tray, spread alternating layers of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, mozarella, meat sauce, parmesan cheese. Repeat these layers.
  7. Cover with foil and make sure it doesn't touch the last layer of cheese.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes. Then remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  9. Rest the lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
