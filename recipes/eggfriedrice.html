Egg Fried Rice

>

Description

Hungry? Make a quick egg fried rice using this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 cup of diced onions
  • 1-2 cups of mixed veggies
  • 1/2 cup of sliced scallions
  • 4 cups of overnight jasmine rice
  • 2.5 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Some salt, black pepper, sesame oil, seasonings

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them together
  2. Heat the skillet with the oil over medium-high heat. Scramble the eggs and take 'em out.
  3. Add onions and the mixed veggies and cook with some salt.
  4. Now add the cooked rice into the pan. Add the seasonings too and mix.
  5. Add the scrambled eggs and garnish with scallions.
