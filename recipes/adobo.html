Adobo

Description

Don't know how to make adobo? Check out this recipe.

Ingredients

    For the marinade:

  • 750g of chicken thigh
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce (not dark)
  • 1/3 cup of white vinegar
  • 4 bay leaves

    • For cooking:

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 small brown onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of black pepper
  • Some scallions

Directions

  1. Combine chickena and marinade ingredients in a bowl for 20 min - overnight.
  2. Keep the marinade. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil on high heat and sear the chicken.
  3. Now cook the garlic and onion.
  4. Add the marinade, water, sugar, and black pepper. Bring the heat down to a medium high and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the chicken and simmer uncovered for 20-25 minutes until sauce reduces to syrupy-sauce.
  6. Serve chicken with glaze over rice.
